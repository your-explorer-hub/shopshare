Signing In

ShopShare uses Google Sign-In for quick and secure access. No separate password required.

  1. Open ShopShare on your Android device. The splash screen appears briefly, then you see the Sign-In page.
  2. Tap "Continue with Google" — a Google account picker appears. Select your account.
  3. Allow permissions if prompted (e.g., microphone for voice input). Tap Allow.
  4. Done! ShopShare automatically creates your personal shopping list on first sign-in.
💡
Your data is securely stored in Firebase and synced to every device where you sign in with the same Google account.

Your First List

After signing in, ShopShare automatically creates a Personal List named MyList_YourName. It's private — only you can see it. Start adding items immediately!

  1. Tap the + button (floating purple button, bottom right of the home screen). The Add Item screen opens.
  2. Type the item name — ShopShare will suggest matching items and auto-detect the category.
  3. Tap "Add to list" — your item appears in the list instantly.

My Lists

Manage your personal and shared shopping lists. Switch between them with a single tap.

Shopping Lists Overview

ShopShare supports two types of lists. You can switch between them at any time using the tab bar at the top of the home screen.

👤 Personal List

Private list only visible to you. Created automatically when you sign up. Named MyList_YourName.

ℹ
You cannot invite others to your personal list. Create a Shared List to collaborate.
👥 Shared List

A collaborative list that multiple family members can view and edit in real time. Synced instantly across all devices.

💡
Named FamilyList_YourName when created.

Switching Between Lists

  1. On the home screen, look at the oval tab bar just below the app title. You'll see My List, Shared, and Search.
  2. Tap "My List" to switch to your private personal list.
  3. Tap "Shared" to switch to your shared family list. If you haven't created or joined one yet, a message will appear.
ℹ
Your last active list is remembered. When you reopen the app, it shows the same list you were on.

Creating a New Shared List

  1. Tap your avatar (top right corner) and select "Invite Member".
  2. On the Manage Members screen, tap "Create a Shared Family List".
  3. Enter a name for your list (e.g., Smith or Holidays). The list will be named FamilyList_Smith.
  4. Tap Create. The app automatically switches to your new shared list.
💡
The Free plan lets you own 1 shared list. Upgrade to Family (3 lists) or Group (10 lists) for more.

Add an Item

Add grocery items, household products, electronics, clothing — anything you need to buy — to your list in seconds.

Adding an Item Manually

  1. Tap the + button (purple floating button at the bottom right of the home screen). The "Add Item" screen opens.
  2. Type the item name in the "Item name" field. Example: Atta, Milk, Headphones, Kurta, Tomatoes.
  3. After 3+ characters, autocomplete suggestions appear. Tap a suggestion to fill it in automatically — the category is set for you.
  4. Choose a category — ShopShare auto-detects the category. You can change it using the dropdown if needed.
  5. Set quantity and unit — Enter the number and choose a unit from the dropdown (pcs, kg, g, L, ml, dozen, box, pack, bunch, pair, bag, bottle, can, strip).
  6. Add notes (optional) — Extra details like brand preference or size (e.g., "Amul only" or "500g pack").
  7. Tap "Add to list" or tap the "Add" button in the top right. The item is added instantly.
💡
Item names are automatically capitalized. ShopShare includes a catalog of 500+ Indian and international item names with smart category detection.

Smart Autocomplete

After 3+ chars, autocomplete shows 500+ item suggestions with category icons.

  • Tap any suggestion to fill name and category automatically
  • Lightbulb icon appears when suggestions are available
  • Info (i) icon means category was auto-detected

Add by Voice

Add items hands-free using speech recognition.

Using Voice Input

  1. Open Voice Input - Tap the microphone icon. A sheet slides up.
  2. Say "Add" - Say "Add", "Add item", or "Please add".
  3. Say the item name - Say it clearly: "Milk", "2 kg Atta".
  4. Review and edit - Confirm the recognized name and category.
  5. Tap "Add to List" - Item is added instantly.
💡
Say it in one go: "Add Milk" and ShopShare skips to confirmation.
  • Include quantities: "3 kg rice", "2 dozen eggs"
  • Filler words ignored: "please", "buy", "get"
  • Tap Retry if recognition is wrong
  • Tap mic again to stop listening
⚠️
Needs microphone permission: Settings > Apps > ShopShare > Permissions > Microphone > Allow.

Edit an Item

Update quantity, unit, or notes on any item.

Edit Quantity and Unit

  1. Tap the quantity chip below the item name (e.g., 2 kg). It has a pencil icon.
  2. A bottom sheet slides up. Enter new quantity and select unit.
  3. Tap the checkmark to save.
💡
Changes sync instantly to all shared list members.

Notes

Notes are set when adding an item. To change notes, delete and re-add the item. Notes appear in italic below the item name.

ℹ️
Full inline editing is planned for a future update.

Move Items

Move items between lists or copy to Today's List.

Select and Move

  1. Tap the checkbox on the right of any item card. Card highlights purple.
  2. Select more items for bulk move if needed.
  3. Tap the Move icon in the toolbar.
DestinationWhat Happens
Today's ListCopies item. Original stays in source list.
Shared ListMoves item. Removed from current, added to shared.

ℹ️

Delete Items

Remove items by swiping, bulk-delete, or clear all completed.

Swipe to Delete

  1. Swipe item left. Red background appears.
  2. Confirm in dialog. Tap Delete.
⚠️
Deletion is permanent. No recycle bin.

Bulk Delete

  1. Tap checkboxes to select items.
  2. Tap the red trash icon in top bar.
  3. Confirm in dialog.

Delete All Completed

  1. Tap the orange sweep icon in top bar.
  2. Confirm how many items to delete.
  3. Tap Delete.
💡
To hide without deleting, use the eye icon instead.
✅

Completed Items

Track bought items, toggle visibility, or clear all.

Mark as Complete

  1. Tap the circle icon on the left of any item. It becomes a checkmark with strikethrough.
  2. Shows who completed it and when.
  3. Tap the checkmark again to uncheck (restore to pending).

Show or Hide Completed

  1. Tap the eye icon in the top bar to toggle visibility.
  2. Eye open = completed items shown. Eye closed = hidden.
💡
Hide completed to focus on what you still need to buy.

Join a Shared List

Enter a List ID to join someone's shared shopping list.

How to Join

Account

Manage sign-out and account deletion. Your data is safely stored in Firebase.

Sign Out

  1. Tap avatar then Sign Out, or go to Settings → Account → Sign Out.
  2. Confirm in the dialog.
  3. You are taken to the login screen. Data stays in cloud.
💡
Sign back in anytime with the same Google account to access all your data.

Delete Account

⚠️
Permanent and irreversible. Deletes all your lists, items, shared lists you own (members lose access), and profile data.
  1. Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  2. Review the warning. Tap Continue.
  3. Type DELETE (all caps) to confirm.
  4. Tap Delete My Account. Done.
ℹ️
If you get a "requires recent login" error, sign out and sign back in first.

FAQ

Common questions and solutions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Items not showing on family member phone?▼
Make sure both are on the Shared tab (not My List). Check internet connection - items sync in real time.
Invited someone but they show as Pending?▼
They need to open ShopShare, go to avatar → Invite Member → Join a Shared List → enter the List ID → tap Join.
Can I use ShopShare offline?▼
Yes! ShopShare has offline support via local SQLite cache. Changes sync automatically when back online.
Accidentally deleted an item. Can I restore it?▼
No. Deleted items are permanent. Tip: use the eye icon to hide completed items instead of deleting them.
Voice input not working?▼
Check microphone permission: Settings → Apps → ShopShare → Permissions → Microphone → Allow. Say Add first, then item name.
Old completed items not showing?▼
History limits: 30 days (Free), 90 days (Family), Unlimited (Group). Upgrade to see older history.
How do I change my display name?▼
Tap avatar → My Profile → pencil icon → update name → Save.
Can I have multiple shopping lists?▼
Yes. 1 personal + shared lists (1 on Free, 3 on Family, 10 on Group).
Items not syncing to other devices?▼
Check internet connection. Ensure same Google account on all devices. Try signing out and back in.
Error creating shared list?▼
You may have reached plan limit. Check Profile for your plan. Upgrade for more lists.
How to send feedback or report a bug?▼
Settings → Support → Feedback / Report Issue → type message → Submit.
